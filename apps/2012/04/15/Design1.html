WaterMe was my first app that was 100% a CRUD app. CRUD basically means that its a database app. It Creates, Reads, Updates, and Deletes database entries. But it displays them in a nice way with really great animations. The premise of the app is that, unlike reminders style apps, this app only gives you one reminder per day letting you know which plants need to be watered that day.
